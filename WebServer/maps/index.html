Allow the user to turn into fire, create fire and control fire. You can fly. All attacks contain a DC 12 Dexterity Saving Throw to half the damage.
  • Heat-Haze (15 Haki): Shoots a flame column in a range of 120 feet straight in one direction the damage is 3d10 for every 10 Haki the user invests more the damage is increased by 1d10. Only one creature can be targeted.
  • Fire-Fist (20 Haki): The user's fist becomes very hot. A forward punch releases a cone of fire that has 15 feet of reach. The fire deals 5d6 damage. The damage increases by 1d6 for 5 haki.
  • Flame-Bid (50 Haki): The user does 3d12 +3 fire damage for 15 Haki in a 20 feet radius, the damage is increased by 1d12.
  • Grand Flame-Bid (100 Haki) (Advarnced): The user does 5d12 +5 fire damage in a 30 feet radius for 15 Haki, the damage is increased by 1d12.
  • Fire-Tower (50 Haki): With this attack, the user turns into a pillar of fire that shoots vertically into the sky. He is even able to incinerate an entire house in one second. The pillar is 100 feet high and 5 feet (self) wide. Creatures standing next to the user suffer no damage. Creatures standing in the flame pillar except the user take 3d100 fire damage.
  • Firefly-Light (60 Haki): The user creates up to 3 fireballs that he can distribute up to 120 feet away from himself. He can explode the fireballs whenever he wants as a bonus action. One fireball does 9d6 fire damage. The user can spend 20 Haki for another fireball.
  • Holy-Fire (10/20 Haki): The user can form one or two fire spears. The user can throw the spears (Strength) (30/120) the spears do 2d6 damage and get stuck in the victim. The spears can be disintegrated by the user, dealing 1d12 fire damage.
  • Fire-Guns (2 Haki/shot): The user can fire small volleys of fire (30/50 Dexterity), these deal 1d4 + 1 damage. In one action the user can fire 3 volleys.
  • Corss-Fire (150 Haki) (Advarnced): The user shoots a flame column that has 100 feet range in one direction. The user must cross fingers of different hands. The user causes 3d100 fire damage to all creatures standing in the column. The user is exhausted after this.
  • Fire-Emperor (500 Haki) (Advarnced): The user needs 5 minutes to perform the technique. The user creates a huge fireball that he can hurl 200 feet. The fireball explodes with a 100 feet sphere and does 10d100 damage to creatures. The user faints afterwards.
