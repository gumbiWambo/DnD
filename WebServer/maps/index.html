Allows the user to transform and create in the darkness. The user of the fruit can cancel other devil powers. The user can pull objects, creatures and buildings into the darkness and release them again. There are also unexplored techniques... Known Weaknesses: The user of the fruit receives 5 times the damage.
  • Black Hole (costs depending by size 1 Haki/Feet by a circle by a sphere 3 Haki/Feet): You spread your darkness over an area depending by the size you choose. When swallowed a object or a creature by the darkness you can store it and subject it to a large gravity (2d12) per round, but you don't have to do that.
  • Liberation (2 Haki/object or creature: You can shoot out what your Black Hole swallowed like a cannon ball. Range: 30 feet if you spend more Haki in it it extends 15 feet per Haki point.
  • Kurouzu (10 Haki): If a creature or object is within 15 feet of you, you can pull the creature or object too you. If the creature or object consumed a devil friut the devil friut has no power any more.
  • Black World (5 Haki) All creatures excluding yourself is blinded in the Black Hole area. This darkness can't be lighted up with any magic.
  • Dark Reflector (5 Haki) If you created a black hole sphere you can use these technique. To prepare an reaction and reflect a missile to any point you like.
  • Dark Walk (passive) You can be invisible in the darkness and you don't need speed to walk around in darkness.
  • Dark Plane (50 Haki) You travell to a dark plane with all creatures who stand in or on a black hole. Your weakness 5 times damage is transferd form you to all other creatures on the plane. The plane exist until the end of your next turn.
